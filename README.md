### 🧠 EthereaLogic.ai — Clarity-First AI Engineering

> *"Is your AI thinking clearly — or just guessing better…?"*

We are an independent AI engineering lab focused on building **practical, high-leverage tools and frameworks** for the agentic AI era. No hype. No bloat. Just systems that reason clearly, run reliably, and scale sensibly.

---

### 👨‍💻 About

Founded by **Anthony Johnson** — systems thinker, AI engineer, and freelance theoretical physicist. At EthereaLogic, we design and release open-source AI infrastructure that prioritizes clarity over complexity, and correctness over cleverness.

We believe the most important problems in AI aren't the flashiest ones — they're the **underexplored, high-leverage ideas** that make everything else work better.
